Features

Posts & Comments

Your site visitors will be updated when a new post or comment has been posted or when an existing one gets edited while they are browsing the respective page.

bbPress Topics

Your site visitors will be updated when a new forum topic has been posted or when an existing one gets edited.

bbPress Replies

Your site visitors will be updated when a new reply for a topic has been posted or when an existing one gets edited.

WooCommerce

Your site visitors will be updated when a the stock of a product changes while they are browsing the product's page.
